# Website Copywriting: [Client Name]

*Use this document to write the complete copywriting. Every word must serve a specific conversion goal. Do not use generic filler words like "passionate", "state-of-the-art", or "world-class".*

---

## Copywriting Guidelines
1. **Specific > Generic:** Write "ceramic coatings that resist 9H scratches" instead of "the best protection for your car."
2. **Focus on Outcomes:** Don't just list what you do; explain how it transforms the customer's state.
3. **No Fluff:** Keep headlines punchy. Ensure every paragraph has high information density.
