## 3. Visual Mood Board Concept
Explain the creative design concept and art direction.

* **Concept Name:** [e.g., Obsidian & Gold / Warm Editorial / Electric Speed]
* **Visual Theme Rationale:**
  > Why does this design concept fit the target audience? (e.g., "High-income car enthusiasts appreciate dark, high-contrast, cinematic styles because it mirrors premium automotive culture.")
* **Image Art Direction:**
  > What style of imagery will we use? (e.g., "No stock photos. High-contrast close-ups of metallic textures, dark moody environments, professional portraits of the team in action.")
* **Key Micro-interactions:**
  > What movement supports this concept? (e.g., "Sleek, slow page reveals; custom mouse cursor transitions; magnetic hover effects on CTAs to make buttons feel premium and interactive.")
